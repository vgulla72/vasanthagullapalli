Welcome

Hi, I’m Vasantha Gullapalli . I’m a senior engineering and product leader with nearly three decades of experience across global services, media and publishing, and large-scale technology platforms. Along the way, I’ve lived and worked in both India and the U.S., which has shaped how I think about leadership, careers, and impact in a global context.

Over the years, I’ve made intentional pivots into entrepreneurship, consulting, and public service. After nearly nine years at Amazon and close to thirty years of nonstop work, I chose to slow down. I wanted more freedom — freedom to reflect, to be deliberate, and to spend my time on work that feels meaningful and aligned.

That pause brought clarity. Certain themes rose to the top and now define where I focus my time — when I’m not traveling, hiking, or spending time with friends and family.

This site is my hub on the internet. It brings together the projects I’m actively building and exploring today. They sit at the intersection of technology, careers, leadership, and lifelong learning. Each is in a different stage of evolution, and I’ll share meaningful updates as they develop.

I invite you to explore. If something resonates and you’d like to exchange perspectives, collaborate, or partner in some way, I’d love to hear from you.

You can also email me directly .

Thanks for being here.
