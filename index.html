Welcome!

Hi, I’m Vasantha Gullapalli . I’m a senior leader in the tech industry with nearly three decades of experience across global services (Publics Sapient), media and publishing(Dow Jones), and large-tech (Amazon). Along the way, I’ve lived and worked in both India and the U.S., which has shaped how I think about leadership, careers, and impact in a global context.

Over the years, I’ve made intentional pivots into entrepreneurship, consulting, and public service. After nearly nine years at Amazon and close to thirty years of nonstop work, I chose to slow down. I wanted more freedom — freedom to reflect, to be deliberate, and to spend my time on things that are meaningful and aligned to who I am.

That pause brought clarity. Some themes rose to the top and now define where I focus my time — when I’m not traveling, hiking, or spending time with friends and family.

This site is my hub on the internet. It brings together the projects I’m currently working on. They sit at the intersection of technology, careers, leadership, life and lifelong learning. Each is in a different stage of evolution, and I’ll share meaningful updates as they develop.

I invite you to explore. If something resonates and you’d like to exchange perspectives, collaborate, or partner in some way, I’d love to hear from you.
