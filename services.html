Professional Services

Since stepping away from Amazon in 2025, I’ve intentionally designed the next phase of my personal and professional life. Alongside long-standing personal interests, I am focused on giving back to the industry by enabling leaders and strengthening organizations.

I am most effective in consulting and fractional leadership roles, where I help organizations scale by developing leadership capability and removing execution bottlenecks through clear, scalable operating mechanisms.

I am selective about engagements and take on work that genuinely excites me. That excitement is usually driven by a combination of the people and the mission.

Areas of Focus

Hiring & Developing Talent

Build data-driven hiring, performance, and talent management processes that raise the talent bar and strengthen leadership pipelines.

Leadership Development

Mentor and develop emerging leaders to build leaders who scale and develop others.

Organization Design

Design organizational structures that balance collaboration with clear accountability.

Operational Excellence

Audit and optimize operating mechanisms to improve execution quality, predictability, and scalability.

1:1 Mentorship

I mentor mid-to-senior leaders in tech, women in tech, and professionals balancing career ambition with the rest of life. My work focuses on helping leaders scale impact in high-pressure engineering environments, navigate transitions, and design careers aligned with what truly matters.

Mentorship and coaching are offered through Topmate.

Background

I began my career as a software engineer and grew into a cross-functional leadership role, building and leading diverse teams across engineering, applied science, data engineering, analytics, product management, and technical program management.

My experience spans global services at Publicis Sapient, media and publishing at Dow Jones (including WSJ.com, Barrons.com, and MarketWatch.com), and large-scale consumer businesses at Amazon. Across these environments, I’ve consistently operated at the intersection of business strategy, technology, and people leadership.

I bring a structured, outcomes-driven approach to diagnosing organizational challenges. I quickly understand business priorities, assess operating landscapes, identify bottlenecks, and translate insights into clear, actionable paths forward.

I’m known as a pragmatic, people-first leader who builds high-performing teams with strong ownership, disciplined execution, and a bias for innovation.

Having lived and worked in both India and the U.S., and balanced a demanding career alongside family responsibilities, I bring a global perspective on leadership, work cultures, and organizational dynamics.
