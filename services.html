Professional Services

Since stepping out of my full-time role at :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} in 2025, I’ve taken time to intentionally design the next phase of my personal and professional life. Alongside pursuing long-standing personal interests, I am now interested in giving back to the industry by enabling leaders and strengthening organizations.

I am most effective in consulting and fractional leadership roles, where I help organizations scale by developing leadership capability and removing execution bottlenecks through clear, scalable operating mechanisms.

I am selective about engagements and take on work that genuinely excites me. That excitement is usually driven by a combination of the people and the mission.

Areas of Focus

  • Hiring & Developing Talent
    Build data-driven hiring, performance, and talent management processes that raise the talent bar and strengthen leadership pipelines.
  • Leadership Development
    Mentor and develop emerging leaders to build leaders who scale and develop others.
  • Organization Design
    Design organizational structures that balance collaboration with clear accountability.
  • Operational Excellence
    Audit and optimize operating mechanisms to improve execution quality, predictability, and scalability.
  • Financial & Planning Processes
    Streamline planning, budgeting, and business review mechanisms to improve alignment, agility, and capital efficiency.

Mentorship

I mentor mid-to-senior leaders in tech, women in tech, and professionals balancing career ambition with the rest of life. My work focuses on helping leaders scale their impact in high-pressure engineering environments, navigate role or industry transitions, and design careers aligned with what truly matters.

Mentorship and coaching are offered through Topmate.

Background

I began my career as a software engineer and grew into a cross-functional leadership role, building and leading diverse teams across engineering, applied science, data engineering, analytics, product management, and technical program management.

My experience spans global services at :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}, media and publishing at :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2} (including WSJ.com, Barrons.com, and MarketWatch.com), and large-scale consumer businesses at Amazon. Across these environments, I’ve consistently operated at the intersection of business strategy, technology, and people leadership.

I bring a structured, outcomes-driven approach to diagnosing organizational challenges. I quickly understand business priorities, assess the technology and operating landscape, identify structural and execution bottlenecks, and translate insights into clear, actionable paths forward.

I’m known as a pragmatic, people-first leader who builds high-performing teams with strong ownership, disciplined execution, and a bias for innovation.

I have also lived the reality of balancing a demanding career with the needs of a young family and aging parents. Having lived and worked in both India and the U.S., I bring a global perspective on leadership, work cultures, and organizational dynamics.
